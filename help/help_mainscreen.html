Quick Scene
Tapping on the console brings up the list of available equipment, and loads the Costumize page for the selected one.
Here you may customize the default values for each module type, and add them to your Favorites. Pressing Done loads the whole gear into the Soundcheck view.
This way you can start saving your work instantly and fill in the details later, using the Clipboard.
Clipboard
The Clipboard contains all your saved gigs and soundchecks. Here you can manage your Sessions, Artists and Venues.
For further details see the Clipboard page.
Settings / Help / About
Tapping the toolbox brings up the Settings. From here, you can also reach the Help and About pages.
